DBT workbooks

Dialectical behavior therapy is useful for establishing emotional regulation and distress tolerance. It can be useful for those with borderline personality disorder. It is also sometimes used to treat bipolar disorder, depression, and anxiety, among other disorders. For a host of reasons, DBT is often even more inaccessible than CBT. There are some public classes that teach DBT in a structured way if you aren’t able to access one-on-one therapy.
