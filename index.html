Advice

BCA has a wellness team that you can go to for therapy if you can't get it outside of school. Their contact info isn't on BCA's site (because of course it isn't), but you can ask either Ms. Sytsma or your guidance counselor to provide you with names/emails. If you're uncomfortable asking directly, it's a good idea to have a friend do it for you.
