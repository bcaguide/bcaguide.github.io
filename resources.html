Where to find what you need

Mental health

BCA has a wellness team (including a psychologist and a dietitian) that you can go to for therapy if you can't get it outside of school. Their contact info isn't on BCA's site (because of course it isn't), but you can ask either Ms. Sytsma or your guidance counselor to provide you with names/emails. If you're uncomfortable asking directly, it's a good idea to have a friend do it for you.

Gender-neutral bathroom + changing room locations

1st floor: Go to the guidance hallway by the front office and across from commons are two gender neutral bathrooms. Use the one on the right, as the left one is locked for staff.

1st floor: Go to the engineering wing and across from Mr. Sam’s room (164), turn right into a smaller hallway with a teacher’s office/lounge and go to the bathroom on the right, as the left one is locked for staff.

Basement: Go to the basement. The gender neutral bathroom + locker room is adjacent to the girl’s locker room, which is between the fitness center and Ms. Pero’s room (6). The door closest to the stairs is the one you need to use. The other door leads straight into the girls’ locker room. Once you go through that first door, continue straight to the gray door, and use that one. The door to the left in that passageway goes to the girls’ locker room. The bathroom is inside the GN locker room.

Resources

BCA-related

SAU website - our website where you can find current and past projects, leadership roles, and how to contact us.

bcar.fun - a place with links for just about every site you may need for official BCA functions (PowerSchool, elective enroll, etc).

yourbcabus.com - where you can you check if your bus has arrived yet.

Homework/Study

lifeat.io - has different environments that can help with studying/focus.

Homework Tracker - a browser extension that can help you organize due dates, subjects and assignments.

BCA Schoology Calendar - a browser extension that allows you to color code the assignments in your calendar by their completion status.

quillbot.com - paraphrasing tool that can help you find a better/more formal way to say things, check your grammar, and check for plagiarism.

Books/Research papers

Library Genesis and Z-Lib - websites where you can download most books, fiction and nonfiction. To use this, it’s often helpful to search by ISBN, which can be found on the book's Amazon page. Z-Lib also has research papers.

Hoopla - an app and website where you can sign in through your library card to access books in a digital library.

Sci-Hub - a website where you can find research papers for free. To use this, it’s helpful to search by DOI number.

The link to log into the New York Times with your school account is here.

The remote login information for databases such as JSTOR is here.
